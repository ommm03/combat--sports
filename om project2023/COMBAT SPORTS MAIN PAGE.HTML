COMBAT SPORTS

A combat sport also referred to as a fighting sport, is a form of competitive contact sport which involves one on one combat. The common methods of winning in these competitions are by scoring more points than the opponents, putting the opponent under some submission hold, knocking them out, or attacking the opponent in a designated or specific manner. Combat sports are believed to share a long pedigree with martial arts.
