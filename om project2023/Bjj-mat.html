What are Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Mats?

Brazilian jiu-jitsu or BJJ MATS, also known as tatami or folding mats, are essential to any martial arts gym.  They provide a safe place to practice takedowns and throws for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners. Not only do BJJ mats provide a cushion during training sessions, but they also protect gym floors from scratches and tears due to the intense activity found in gyms.


Different Types of BJJ Mats

  • Folding mats are great if you're travelling or you don't want to use all the time. These can be quite costly and you require adequate storage.

  • Smooth mats are great for doing partner drills.

  • Puzzle mats offer more of a traditional training feel and a simple to install with the interlocking design.

  • Tatami mats create a resistant surface but also have plenty of giving to reduce impact and protect joints.

  • Roll-out mats are excellent for portability and taking to events and competitions but they require alot of storage space.
